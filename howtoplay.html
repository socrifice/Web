How To Play and Earn PCS


Making gaming easy and rewarding is our top priority. Therefore, owning a Phantom Wallet isn't necessarily crucial for playing the game. However, if you find yourself enjoying the experience, feel free to use a Phantom Wallet for prize withdrawals. Follow the steps below to get yourSolana Wallet by Phantom.


Install Phantom Wallet:

If you haven't installed the Phantom wallet extension, you can do so by visiting the Chrome Web Store or the Firefox Add-ons page and searching for "Phantom Wallet". Add the extension to your browser.


Create or Import a Wallet:

Open the Phantom extension, and either create a new wallet or import an existing one by following the on-screen instructions.


Fund Your Wallet:

Ensure that your Phantom wallet has sufficient SOL (Solana) tokens to cover transaction fees and interact with DApps. You can deposit SOL into your wallet from a cryptocurrency exchange.




THE GAME



Visit pieces.network:

Open your Phantom wallet web browser and go to the pieces.network website.


Connect Phantom Wallet:

Sign Up for create an account or you can login if you have username, Look for a "Connect Wallet" or similar button on the pieces.network website. Click on it.


Authorize Connection:

A popup from Phantom Wallet will appear, asking for authorization. Confirm and authorize the connection between your Phantom wallet and pieces.network.


Explore pieces.network:

Once connected, you can explore the features and functionalities of the pieces.network website. This may include accessing Play-to-Earn games, checking tokenomics, and more.


Make Transactions:

If the pieces.network website involves transactions (such as purchasing or trading PCS), use your Phantom wallet to confirm and complete the transactions.


Congratulations! You've successfully connected your Phantom wallet to pieces.network. Enjoy exploring the platform and engaging with its content.
